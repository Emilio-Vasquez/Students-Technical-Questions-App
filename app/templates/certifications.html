Industry Certifications

The UCNJ Cyber Center proudly supports students pursuing industry-recognized certifications that enhance career readiness in Cybersecurity and Data Science. Below you'll find the current certifications we offer, along with guidance on eligibility and preparation.

CompTIA Security+

Coordinator: Nahla Al Hamad (nahla.alhamad@ucc.edu)
Track: Cybersecurity
Eligibility: Open to students in their second year (sophomore) at UCNJ who are actively pursuing cybersecurity-related studies.
Details: The CompTIA Security+ certification validates baseline skills necessary to perform core security functions and pursue an IT security career.

CompTIA Data+

Coordinator: Emilio Vasquez (emilio.vasquez@ucc.edu)
Track: Data Science
Eligibility: Open to students majoring in Data Science. Approval is required from Emilio Vasquez prior to registration.
Details: The CompTIA Data+ certification demonstrates foundational knowledge in data analytics, including data mining, visualization, and governance.

Not sure where to start? Reach out to Nahla or Emilio for personalized guidance on which certification aligns best with your academic path and career goals.
